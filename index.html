About Us

PHT intergrated farms is a poultry farm with breeding operation and processing of commercial Broiler, We offer some of the best poultry products in the market along with mutton and fish to consumers. We also deal with the distribution of vital feed to our various customers who are also involved in the poultry, fish, and ruminant aspects of farming.

The vital feed brand is essential to farmers who wish to increase their productivity.
